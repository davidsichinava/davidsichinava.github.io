About

I’m David Sichinava, human geographer with more than ten years of experience in applied social research and policy analysis. Currently, I serve as Research Director at CRRC-Georgia. I also work as assistant professor at the Department of Human Geography at Tbilisi State University and as an invited lecture at the International School of Economics (ISET).
